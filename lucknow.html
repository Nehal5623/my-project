Lucknow


    The Land of Nawabs and Awadhi cuisine, Lucknow is a total foodie's
    paradise! From kebabs to paans to biryanis, wander around in the 
    city for a day or two and you'll never know which narrow alley 
    takes you to food heaven. The taste, the smell and the air of the 
    city screams magic!
